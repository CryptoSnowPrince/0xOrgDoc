Frequently Asked
Questions

What's a private space?

A private project that only you and your team can access. Imagine a space like a folder for your docs.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept Visa, American Express and Mastercard credit & debit cards. All payments are securely processed by Stripe.

Are prices the same worldwide?

Yes, your account will be charged in US Dollars. Local prices may change as exchange rates fluctuate.

What's a user?

A GitBook user is any user that can login to GitBook and exists in user management.

What's a guest?

Guests are members that have no default organization role. They need to have their permissions set explicitly at a content level. They count as a paid seat.

Do paid plans have a minimum contract?

GitBook plans are monthly or annually with no contract. You can cancel or change plan at any time.

Do you offer non profit/open source discounts?

Yes, GitBook is free forever for non profit / open source projects.
